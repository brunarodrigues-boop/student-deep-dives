Deep Dive by Subject

Reading
Platform: ClearFluency  ·  Average: 20 min/day  ·  ~80% unproductive

Leo averages 20 minutes per school day in ClearFluency; however, approximately 80% of this time is unproductive due to ineffective usage patterns. Their fluency is currently at the minimum threshold for engaging with 2nd-grade content.

Why Leo is not learning 2×

Inconsistent engagement: Leo used ClearFluency on only 9 of the last 15 school days. To remain on track for XP goals, Leo must master at least 2 stories per day.
Rushing through comprehension quizzes: Quizzes are often completed in ~1 minute each — insufficient time to read questions thoroughly, check answer options, and return to the passage for evidence. As a result, 50% of quizzes had zero correct answers.
Fluency at minimum threshold: Leo's fluency is at the floor for 2nd-grade content. Recordings also suggest a possible rhotacism (substitution of the "r" sound with "w").

Going Forward

Placement adjustment test assigned: Leo has been assigned a ClearFluency placement adjustment test to diagnose gaps in phonics, phonemic awareness, decoding, and comprehension, and to recalibrate their library to the correct difficulty level. Identified gaps will be addressed through a custom plan in Lalilo and ANTON.
Microphone settings adjustment: Leo's microphone settings will be adjusted to slightly reduce pronunciation strictness. This will support progress despite a mild speech impediment without bypassing proper reading requirements.
Coaching recommended: Academic coaching support in ClearFluency is recommended to address Leo's tendency to rush through comprehension quizzes and to strengthen careful reading habits.
Math
Platform: Synthesis Tutor → Zearn  ·  Average: 34 min/day  ·  ~60% unproductive

Leo averages 34 minutes per school day in Synthesis Tutor; however, approximately 60% of this time is unproductive. Leo has worked through addition, subtraction, and place value up to fractions, multiplication, and division over the last couple of months.

Why Leo is not learning 2×

Out-of-order lesson navigation: Leo did not follow the recommended lesson order in Synthesis Tutor, leading to engagement with concepts above their current skill level. Progress has been lower than expected, and time spent has not consistently translated into XP.
Calculator use: Leo used a calculator to solve multiplication questions in Zearn, bypassing the skill-building the platform is designed to develop.

Going Forward

Transitioned to Zearn: Leo has been moved to Zearn to ensure skills are built sequentially. This will allow Leo to engage at the appropriate level and demonstrate mastery before advancing.
Structured pathway: Leo will follow a sequential learning pathway to avoid skipping ahead to concepts that require foundational skills not yet in place.
Fast Math
Current: 3 XP/day  ·  Target: 10 XP/day  ·  Accuracy: ~60% on single-digit addition

Leo is currently earning significantly less XP per day than expected in Fast Math (3 XP/day vs. a goal of 10). Accuracy and efficiency are well below expectations, limiting fluency development.

Why Leo is not learning 2×

Low accuracy: Leo's accuracy remains low even on single-digit addition (~60%).
Quitting assessments: Leo has at least 3 assessments with a 0 speed score, and multiple more with a speed score of 1 or 2 — typically indicating minimal effort or quitting. Leo also frequently quits assessments halfway through.

Going Forward

App coaching sessions recommended: Coaching sessions in Fast Math are recommended to help Leo learn how to properly use the program and build productive habits. Emphasis on completing assessments fully and maintaining focus, with direct attention to the pattern of quitting or disengaging.
Daily minimum: Leo should complete at least 10 minutes of focused practice daily in Fast Math to build fluency and work toward earning 10 XP per day.
Language
Platform: Lalilo  ·  Average: 20 min/day  ·  Target: 25 min/day  ·  ~30% unproductive

Leo averages 20 minutes per school day in Lalilo — below the 25-minute daily target — with approximately 30% of that time unproductive due to distraction and ineffective usage patterns.

Why Leo is not learning 2×

Below time target: Leo is putting in 20% less time than required (20 min vs. 25 min target).
Closing or minimizing the app: Leo frequently minimizes or closes the app, causing idle time and lost progress. For example, accidentally closing a lesson caused 2.5 minutes of downtime — approximately 10% of active time wasted in that session.
Guessing before reading: Leo guesses answers before reading the question and is distracted by talking while completing quizzes.
Navigating away mid-session: Leo leaves lessons mid-session and spends time talking instead of focusing, further reducing productive learning time.

Going Forward

Increase daily time: Leo needs to consistently reach 25 minutes per school day in Lalilo. With consistent time input and by eliminating distracted learning, Leo will reach their 2× XP goals.
Coaching recommended: Academic coaching while using Lalilo is recommended to reduce the anti-patterns of idling, distraction, and rushing.
