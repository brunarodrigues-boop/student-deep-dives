Anti-Patterns Identified

The following recurring behavioral patterns were observed this week and are impacting both learning quality and time efficiency.

1. Not staying focused on instructional content
Alita repeatedly disengaged from lessons, leading to repeated errors and getting stuck on content she had not absorbed.
2. Rushing or skipping through instructional videos
Videos were skipped rather than watched fully. This is a primary driver of downstream errors — she is attempting quizzes without completing the instruction they are based on.
3. Rushing through questions without careful reading
Answers submitted without thinking through the question, without double-checking, and without using pen and paper for multi-step problems. This caused careless errors and wasted time looping back.
4. Not engaging with correction lessons (boosts)
When correction lessons were assigned after mistakes, Alita did not engage with them meaningfully. This means she repeated the same errors without improvement.
5. Idling, off-task behaviour, and leaving the workspace
Frequent pauses, conversations with others, leaving the screen, and sidetracking with unrelated activities. This accounts for a significant share of her logged time this week.
6. Exiting the app before finishing lessons
Lessons were exited mid-session, causing progress to be lost and the same content to be repeated on return — a direct time and XP drain.

Coaching Recommendations for Alita

The following coaching points should be delivered to Alita directly by her guide this week. Each point maps to a specific anti-pattern above.

1. Focus and listen during lessons
Pay close attention and listen carefully to the lessons so you don't make a lot of mistakes or get stuck. Watch the whole videos without skipping and take your time to learn and understand the content — these will help you do better on the Tower of Power quizzes.
2. Slow down and read questions carefully
Take your time — don't rush. Read the questions carefully and make sure you understand what they are asking. Remember and use what you've learned from the lessons. Count and solve carefully, use your pen and paper to work step by step so you don't miss anything. Double-check your answers before you submit.
3. Look at diagrams and check your work
Look closely at pictures or diagrams and match them with what the question says. Check numbers and signs carefully to make sure your solutions and answers are correct.
4. Use correction lessons to get better
When you make a mistake, focus on the lesson that shows the correction — this will help you get it right next time. These lessons are there to help you, not slow you down.
5. Stay focused and avoid distractions
Stay active and keep working in the app without long breaks or pauses. Don't leave your workspace or talk to other people while studying. Keep your eyes on the screen and use your time to finish and master your assigned lessons.
6. Finish lessons before closing
Finish your lessons before leaving the app so you can earn XP and keep moving forward. If you exit early, you may have to repeat parts when you come back — this wastes time and slows your progress.
